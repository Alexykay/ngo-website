VISION


To build resilient and inclusive communities where people thrive in harmony with nature; advancing sustainable development, restoring ecosystems and securing a just and climate resilient future.


MISSION


To work alongside communities to drive sustainable development through education, environmental restoration, livelihood support and strategic partnerships - while contributing to climate action and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).


Founded in March 2026, Sankofa Earth Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization in Ghana committed to advancing women and children empowerment, environmental sustainability and community resilience.
The Foundation works closely with schools, rural communities, youth groups and local stakeholders to create opportunities that promote education, climate awareness, leadership development and sustainable livelihoods. Through community-driven initiatives, Sankofa Earth Foundation aims to empower vulnerable populations, strengthen resilience to social and environmental challenges and inspire the next generation to become active leaders of positive change.
